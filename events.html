Events

conducted so far...

Huge Smiles, Little Hearts with RHA

21st & 22nd Nov'2023

In our 2nd Offline Activity, we partnered with the Robin Hood Army in Indore, where we organized a painting competition for 30 underprivileged children and provided stationary items to all of them and some sweets to the winners. Our participants, especially females, shared valuable insights on important topics. The experience, combined with city exploration, created a truly memorable offline trip.

Guest Session with
Peepal Farm's Founder Robin Singh

16th Oct'2023

We conducted our guest session with none other than Robin Singh, founder of Peepal Farm. He is a computer scientist and entrepreneur turned into a social worker & created an Animal Welfare Community of India. With his practical approached and minimum suffering based lifestyle he is inspiring millions of Indians. We asked all our animal welfare related doubts and got practical advices from him. As he has a huge following on social media almost all the attendees knew him beforehand.

Poster Making Competition

1st to 31st Aug 2023

We conducted this poster making competition in collaboration with Diplomacia society following our one of the goal of conduction social issues related completions. The posters we received showcased the hidden talent of our student community and also highlighted the burning issues existing in the society with a flavour of art making and poster making. The winners were awarded with certificates and appreciation.

A Day in Animal Aid Unlimited

1st & 2nd Oct'2023

In our 1st Offline Activity, we collaborated with Animal Aid Unlimited India (AAU) in Udaipur. With 30+ students, we volunteered with AAU, exploring Udaipur's iconic sites. Guided by AAU, we learned compassionate animal handling, from Lake Pichola to Bahubali Hills. The experience created lasting memories, bonds, and inspired us to make a positive impact.

RAKSHAK: A Session with
Major Sidram Solankar (NSG Commando)

14th Aug'2023

We conducted the our very first event in collaboration with Kavach club of Bandipur house where we had a thrilling conversation with Major Sidram Solankar sir who is an EX-Army Personel and NSG Commando and we deep dived into the life of a NSG commando and a true patriot. From technical questions related to indian military and nsg to general life questions sir answered them all.

Orientation Session of Sahara society

24th July'2023

We inaugrated our society by conducting an Orientation session and started this journey. We stated some goals and functioning of sahara society there. Also had discussion and doubt session with the interested members who later became our permanent members. Almost 100 people attended the live orientation and 180+ people watched it on our YouTube channel.
